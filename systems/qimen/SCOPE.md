# Qimen v0.3 scope

This slice calculates a timestamped `shijia-zhuanpan-chaibu-full-v0.3` structural chart: normalized time, current solar term, yin/yang dun, upper/middle/lower yuan, ju number, nine-palace earth and heaven plates, hour xun leader, hidden Jia stem, nine stars, eight doors, eight spirits, duty star/door placement (including disclosed center hosting), void branches, selected three-Qi tomb, instrument-punishment, and door-oppression flags.

v0.3 traces shared plate vocabulary to 《奇門寶鑑御定》 and adds explicit `civil` or `apparent_solar` time selection with longitude-based correction metadata.

The plate fixes one source-traceable rotation and center-hosting policy; alternative schools require
separate lineage IDs and cases. It does not choose event 用神, interpret formations, select
auspicious directions, or predict outcomes and timing. The output is a complete bounded structural
plate, not a practitioner-approved judgment product.
