# Known Qimen disputes

- `DSP-QIMEN-JU-METHOD-001`: Chaibu versus Zhirun ju selection. v0.1 implements only Chaibu and rejects silent substitution.
- Rotating-plate, flying-plate, Yin-pan, day, hour, and minute methods are different lineages.
- Solar-term time basis, true-solar-time correction, center-palace hosting, and duty rotation contain additional school choices not implemented here.
- v0.1 is a foundation record, not a complete Qimen pan or direction-selection product.
