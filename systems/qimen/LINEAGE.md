# Qimen lineage

`shijia-zhuanpan-chaibu-v0.1` fixes the method to time-based Shijia Qimen, rotating-plate terminology, and the Chaibu three-yuan ju table. Solar terms are evaluated as instants using the project's pinned calendar dependency and compared in China Standard Time. The caller's civil time is first normalized from an IANA zone.

Yang dun runs the nine earth-plate stems forward from the ju palace; yin dun runs them backward. The order is 戊己庚辛壬癸丁丙乙. The hour's sexagenary xun identifies the hidden Jia instrument and its original duty-star and duty-door palace. This engineering transcription remains pending independent practitioner acceptance.
