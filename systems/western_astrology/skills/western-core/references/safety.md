# Interpretation boundary

Treat signs, houses, and aspects as symbolic vocabulary. Do not convert a placement into a diagnosis, identity fact, compatibility verdict, moral judgment, or guaranteed event. Keep astronomical facts separate from low-confidence commentary and cite fact/rule IDs for every statement.

Do not add unsupported dignities, nodes, Placidus cusps, progressions, transits, synastry, or timing. For medical, legal, financial, safety, employment, fertility, or mortality questions, route to real-world evidence and qualified professional help.
