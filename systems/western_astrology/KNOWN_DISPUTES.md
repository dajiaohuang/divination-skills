# Known Western astrology disputes

- `DSP-WESTERN-HOUSE-SYSTEM-001`: whole-sign versus equal houses. v0.1 defaults to whole sign and requires the selected system in normalized input.
- v0.1 is tropical and geocentric. Sidereal zodiacs, topocentric positions, alternative ayanamsas, and heliocentric charts are separate lineages.
- Major aspect types and orbs are fixed project configuration. Practitioner-specific orbs or minor aspects require separate rules.
- Angle and house calculations are engineering implementations pending independent professional-software comparison.
