# Known limitations

- Astronomy Engine targets approximately arcminute-class general astronomy, not parity with every professional astrology ephemeris.
- Whole-sign and equal houses do not reproduce Placidus or other quadrant-house software.
- High-latitude angle behavior needs a larger independent comparator set.
- Fixed aspect orbs are a product policy; other schools use body-specific and applying/separating orbs.
- No classical or modern interpretive school has expert production approval yet.
- Secondary progressions, directions, composites, and horary are not implemented.
- Transits and solar returns are structural only and do not predict events.
- Synastry does not score compatibility or infer intent, fidelity, or outcome.
- Rectification ranks intervals with dated-event training and holdout sets; it cannot prove a unique
  birth minute and may return `underdetermined`.
- Reader and validator accept structured JSON/CSV only, not OCR or PDF.
- Traditional conditions cover only domicile, detriment, exaltation, and fall table membership; triplicity, terms, faces, reception, sect, accidental dignity, and scoring are absent.
