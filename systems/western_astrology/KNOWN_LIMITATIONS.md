# Known limitations

- Astronomy Engine targets approximately arcminute-class general astronomy, not parity with every professional astrology ephemeris.
- Whole-sign and equal houses do not reproduce Placidus or other quadrant-house software.
- High-latitude angle behavior needs a larger independent comparator set.
- Fixed aspect orbs are a product policy; other schools use body-specific and applying/separating orbs.
- No classical or modern interpretive school has expert production approval yet.
