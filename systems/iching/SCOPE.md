# I Ching v0.1 scope

This slice supports a reproducible three-coin cast, six bottom-to-top line values, moving-line detection, primary and changed hexagrams, trigram decomposition, the conventional King Wen number and name, and a bounded structural report.

It does not reproduce classical translations or commentaries, select one disputed moving-line interpretation method, add nuclear or mutual hexagrams, claim predictive validity, or substitute for high-impact professional advice.
