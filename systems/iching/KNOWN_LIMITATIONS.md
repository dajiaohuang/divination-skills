# Known limitations

- The deterministic seed protocol provides replayability, not proof of divinatory validity.
- Project themes are newly authored and do not replace a named classical translation.
- v0.1 reports all moving lines but does not choose among school-specific moving-line priority rules.
- The King Wen mapping and coin convention require independent practitioner acceptance before a production release claim.
- Questions are represented only by a SHA-256 digest in output; operators must still avoid entering sensitive data unnecessarily.
