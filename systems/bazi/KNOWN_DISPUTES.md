Machine-readable dispute records in `disputes/` are authoritative. The initial set covers:

- `DSP-BAZI-DAY-BOUNDARY-001`: midnight versus 23:00 day change;
- `DSP-BAZI-SOLAR-TIME-001`: civil time versus true solar time;
- `DSP-BAZI-LUCK-DIRECTION-001`: explicit direction versus gender/year-stem inference;
- `DSP-BAZI-LUCK-START-001`: luck-cycle start-age conversion methods;
- `DSP-BAZI-MONTH-BOUNDARY-001`: exact term instant versus whole-day boundary.

The product default is a documented engineering selection, not a declaration that other lineages are wrong.
