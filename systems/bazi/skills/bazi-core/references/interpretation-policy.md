# Interpretation policy

Allowed v0.1 content:

- identify pillars, stems, branches, elements, polarity, hidden stems, and day master;
- explain that a Ten God is a relational label computed from the day stem;
- identify combinations, clashes, harms, and punishments already present in `computed_facts`;
- present luck-cycle pillars and start ages as method-specific calculated data;
- compare documented boundary policies.

Not yet allowed:

- strong/weak day-master claims;
- structure, transformation, climate adjustment, useful or unfavorable gods;
- career, wealth, marriage, health, fertility, death, litigation, or investment predictions;
- retrospective fitting of user history to chart symbols;
- a claim that symbolic output proves causation or inevitability.

Use “the chart contains”, “this rule labels”, and “under the selected method”. Avoid “you are”, “you will”, and fixed dates unless a reviewed timing rule and case support them.
