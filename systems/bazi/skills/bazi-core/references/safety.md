# Safety

Treat Bazi as symbolic or reflective guidance, not evidence for high-stakes decisions.

- Do not diagnose health or mental-health conditions.
- Do not advise stopping treatment or replacing professional care.
- Do not give legal, investment, credit, employment, insurance, or immigration decisions based on a chart.
- Do not predict death, serious illness, pregnancy outcome, crime, or unavoidable disaster.
- Do not infer protected or sensitive traits.
- For distress, coercion, abuse, or self-harm signals, prioritize immediate real-world safety and qualified help over divination.

Offer lower-risk framing: options, questions to reflect on, reversible next steps, and the method's uncertainty.
