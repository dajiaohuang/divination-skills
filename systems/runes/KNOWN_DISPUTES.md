# Known rune disputes

- `DSP-RUNES-REVERSALS-001`: upright-only project baseline versus modern reversed or merkstave meanings. v0.1 rejects reversals.
- Rune order, names, transliterations, and inclusion of blank runes vary among modern products. v0.1 fixes 24 Elder Futhark identifiers and includes no blank rune.
- Historical inscription meanings and modern divinatory keywords are kept conceptually separate.
- Other futharks or reconstructed ritual claims require new lineage and source review.
