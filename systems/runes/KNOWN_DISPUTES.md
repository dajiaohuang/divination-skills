# Known rune disputes

- `DSP-RUNES-REVERSALS-001`: upright-only project baseline versus modern reversed or merkstave meanings. v0.2 rejects reversals.
- `DSP-RUNES-HISTORY-MEANING-001`: standards- and artifact-backed grapheme identity versus modern divination meanings. v0.3 forbids back-projecting the latter onto historical evidence.
- Rune order, names, transliterations, and inclusion of blank runes vary among modern products. v0.2 fixes 24 Elder Futhark identifiers and includes no blank rune.
- Historical inscription meanings and modern divinatory keywords are kept conceptually separate.
- Other futharks or reconstructed ritual claims require new lineage and source review.
