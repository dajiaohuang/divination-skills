# Runes v0.3 scope

Text-only 24-rune Elder Futhark baseline with single and three-position draws. The output keeps public-domain historical/linguistic source metadata strictly separate from project-authored modern reflection prompts. Reversals and blank runes are excluded because their use is not universal. No rune art beyond Unicode names, magical claims, historical reconstruction claims, guaranteed events, or high-impact advice.

v0.3 adds the Unicode character, scalar value, standardized name, and transliteration for each rune, corroborated against the Swedish History Museum's Kylver stone record of a complete 24-character older futhark.
