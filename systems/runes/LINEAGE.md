# Runes lineage

`elder-futhark-project-v0.1` uses the 24-rune Elder Futhark order and project-authored modern reflective keywords. It explicitly distinguishes modern divinatory use from historical evidence. Three positions are `context`, `tension`, and `practice`.
`anglo-saxon-rune-name-evidence-v0.2` records Old English cognate names and a public-domain
historical locator only. `elder-futhark-project-reflection-v0.2` contains project-authored modern
keywords. Historical evidence never serves as a citation for modern divinatory meanings, and the
modern layer never claims reconstructed historical practice.
