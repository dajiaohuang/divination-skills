# Known limitations

- Grand Tableau support is limited to 4×9 coordinates, houses, and explicit significator location; there is no expert-approved pair dictionary or advanced grammar.
- Man and Woman remain conventional card names but never identify gender or a person automatically.
- Text-only project keywords are reflective prompts, not predictive evidence.
- Grand Tableau output is a 4×9 project layout; alternate 8×4+4 layouts are not silently mixed.
- Houses and coordinate relations are not predictive or factual claims.
