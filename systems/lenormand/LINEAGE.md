# Lenormand lineage

`lenormand-36-project-v0.3` preserves conventional card numbers and names while using original project keywords. Three-card positions are `context`, `development`, and `focus`. Nine-card positions are row-major `p1` through `p9`.
v0.2 adds ordered adjacent pairs, an explicit 3×3 nine-card geometry, and a 4×9 Grand Tableau
with fixed houses numbered 1–36. Optional Man or Woman significators produce coordinate
differences only. Reversals remain excluded and no geometric relation is an outcome claim.

`game-of-hope-identity-v0.3` supplies historical card numbers and French playing-card miniatures from the surviving complete pack. It does not supply the modern interpretation keywords.
