# Lenormand lineage

`lenormand-36-project-v0.1` preserves conventional card numbers and names while using original project keywords. Three-card positions are `context`, `development`, and `focus`. Nine-card positions are row-major `p1` through `p9`; v0.1 does not infer diagonals, knighting, mirrors, houses, or person-card identity.
v0.2 adds ordered adjacent pairs, an explicit 3×3 nine-card geometry, and a 4×9 Grand Tableau
with fixed houses numbered 1–36. Optional Man or Woman significators produce coordinate
differences only. Reversals remain excluded and no geometric relation is an outcome claim.
