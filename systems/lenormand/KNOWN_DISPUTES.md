# Known Lenormand disputes

- `DSP-LENORMAND-REVERSALS-001`: traditional upright-only handling versus modern reversal extensions. v0.1 is upright-only and rejects reversal input.
- Person cards are generic role markers; v0.1 does not infer sex, gender identity, or a real person's hidden attributes.
- Grand Tableau houses, mirroring, knighting, distance, and card-pair dictionaries are outside the current three supported layouts.
- Alternative deck numbering or guidebook wording requires a distinct lineage and licensed source.
