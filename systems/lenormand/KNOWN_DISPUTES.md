# Known Lenormand disputes

- `DSP-LENORMAND-REVERSALS-001`: traditional upright-only handling versus modern reversal extensions. v0.2 is upright-only and rejects reversal input.
- `DSP-LENORMAND-HISTORY-MEANING-001`: historical card identity versus modern reading meanings. v0.3 records them as separate evidence layers.
- Person cards are generic role markers; v0.2 does not infer sex, gender identity, or a real person's hidden attributes.
- v0.2 implements 4×9 Grand Tableau houses only; alternate layouts, mirroring, knighting, distance, and lineage-specific pair dictionaries remain separate.
- Alternative deck numbering or guidebook wording requires a distinct lineage and licensed source.
