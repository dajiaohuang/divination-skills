# Safety boundary

- Symbols are reflective prompts, not evidence or professional advice.
- Do not diagnose, prescribe, determine legal strategy, recommend financial transactions, assess danger, or predict death or pregnancy.
- Do not claim access to another person's thoughts, fidelity, identity, location, or intentions.
- In crisis or coercive situations, prioritize immediate real-world safety resources and qualified help.
- Prefer reversible questions and identify which external evidence would reduce uncertainty.
