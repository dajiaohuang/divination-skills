# Known limitations

- The keywords are a compact project baseline, not a substitute for lineage-specific expert practice.
- Randomness is auditable and reproducible when the seed is disclosed; no software-only draw can prove metaphysical randomness.
- Question text is hashed and omitted from structured output by default, so later interpretation needs the caller to retain its own context.
- Combination analysis is limited to direct adjacency and descriptive element/number/suit distributions; elemental dignities and complex grammars are not implemented.
- No images are distributed; visual-symbol analysis is unavailable.
- No card images, commercial guidebook text, or modern deck assets are included.
- Journal files may contain sensitive user-authored reflections and remain local by default.
- Journal statistics do not measure predictive validity or accuracy.
- The Waite source establishes the selected RWS identity, not universal meanings across Tarot lineages.
