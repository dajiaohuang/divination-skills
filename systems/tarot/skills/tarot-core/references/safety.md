# Tarot safety boundary

- State that cards are symbolic prompts, not evidence.
- Do not diagnose, prescribe, determine legal strategy, recommend trades or loans, assess danger, or predict death or pregnancy.
- Do not claim to reveal another person's thoughts, fidelity, location, identity, or intentions.
- For coercive or crisis situations, prioritize immediate real-world safety resources and qualified help.
- Offer reversible reflection questions and identify what external evidence would reduce uncertainty.
