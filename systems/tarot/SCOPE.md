# Tarot v0.1 scope

The baseline is a text-only Rider–Waite–Smith-compatible 78-card ontology with project-authored keywords. It supports auditable single-card and three-card draws, optional reversals, fixed position semantics, reproducible entropy, and bounded reflective explanations.

It does not include card images, copy modern deck guidebooks, predict guaranteed events, answer high-impact medical/legal/financial questions, infer hidden facts about third parties, or claim that randomized cards are empirical evidence. Marseille, Thoth, oracle decks, custom spreads, journaling, and user outcome tracking are outside v0.1.
