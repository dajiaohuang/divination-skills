# Tarot v0.3 scope

The baseline is a text-only Rider–Waite–Smith-compatible 78-card ontology with project-authored keywords. It supports auditable single-card, three-card, elemental-five, relationship-six, horseshoe-seven, and Celtic Cross draws; optional reversals; fixed position semantics; reproducible entropy; bounded reflective explanations; adjacency/distribution summaries; and a consent-gated local journal with descriptive statistics.

v0.3 attaches RWS arcana, deck index, rank, suit, element, and identity lineage to every card fact with a public-domain Waite source locator. Images and source prose remain excluded.

It does not include card images, copy modern deck guidebooks, predict guaranteed events, answer high-impact medical/legal/financial questions, infer hidden facts about third parties, or claim that randomized cards are empirical evidence. Marseille, Thoth, oracle decks, arbitrary custom spreads, predictive outcome scoring, and cloud journal storage are outside v0.2.
