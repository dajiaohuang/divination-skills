# Known limitations

- No independent Ziwei practitioner has yet accepted the native Golden Cases.
- The current star set is limited to fourteen major stars and four structural minor stars.
- Birth minutes only determine the explicit double-hour block; minute-level star placement is not
  claimed.
- True solar time and longitude correction are not applied.
- Interpretation, 流年/流月, 合盘, auxiliary star expansion, and school-difference matrices remain
  outside v0.1.
- iztro is an ignored comparison reference only and cannot be used to fill missing runtime facts.
