# Known Liuyao disputes

- `DSP-LIUYAO-DAY-BOUNDARY-001`: civil-midnight versus late-Zi day change. v0.1 defaults to midnight and accepts `zi_initial` only when explicit.
- 用神 classification, 旺衰 precedence, 进退神, 伏神, and timing methods vary by lineage and are deliberately absent.
- The eight-palace, 纳甲, 世应, 六亲, 六神, and 旬空 tables are frozen as an engineering transcription pending practitioner review.
- General I Ching text reading and timestamped Wen Wang Liuyao must not be silently combined.
