# Numerology v0.1 scope

The baseline calculates Life Path, Birthday, Expression, Soul Urge, Personality, and Maturity numbers using an explicit Pythagorean A–Z mapping and master numbers 11, 22, and 33. Input names are Unicode-normalized but must reduce to Latin A–Z letters.

Chaldean mappings, Chinese stroke/name systems, transliteration, legal-name selection, compound-number traditions, forecasts, compatibility, and deterministic event claims are excluded. Results are project-authored reflective labels, not evidence about identity or aptitude.
