# Known numerology disputes

- `DSP-NUMEROLOGY-MAPPING-001`: Pythagorean repeating 1–9 mapping versus Chaldean or other mappings. v0.1 supports only the named Pythagorean baseline.
- Master-number retention policies vary. v0.1 retains 11, 22, and 33 at every reduction step.
- Name choice, punctuation, titles, transliteration, and non-Latin scripts are unresolved product decisions; v0.1 never guesses transliteration.
- Date formats are normalized to Gregorian ISO dates and no alternative calendar is used.
