# Known numerology disputes

- `DSP-NUMEROLOGY-MAPPING-001`: Pythagorean repeating 1–9 mapping versus Chaldean or other mappings. v0.2 supports two isolated named mappings and never combines them.
- Master-number retention policies vary. The Pythagorean method retains 11, 22, and 33; the Chaldean method does not inherit that policy.
- Name choice, punctuation, titles, transliteration, and non-Latin scripts are explicit product decisions; v0.2 never guesses transliteration.
- Date formats are normalized to Gregorian ISO dates and no alternative calendar is used.
