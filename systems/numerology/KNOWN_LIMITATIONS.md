# Known limitations

- Non-Latin names require a separately reviewed transliteration policy and are rejected.
- Name changes, multiple surnames, initials, titles, and chosen-versus-legal names are not adjudicated.
- Number themes are low-confidence symbolic prompts and have no validated predictive meaning.
- The Cheiro source establishes the selected historical mapping, not scientific validity or universal Chaldean practice.
- Transliteration is never inferred; user-supplied spelling materially changes the result.
- Mixed-script names require a complete user-supplied Latin transliteration.
