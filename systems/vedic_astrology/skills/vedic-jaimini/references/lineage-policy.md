# Jaimini policy

The seven-kāraka policy ranks Sun through Saturn by descending degree within a
sign. The eight-kāraka policy adds Rāhu and uses `30° - degree_in_sign` as its
effective arc. Exact ties are rejected.

Movable signs aspect non-adjacent fixed signs; fixed signs aspect non-adjacent
movable signs; dual signs aspect the other dual signs.

Ārūḍha lagna repeats the distance from lagna to its lord from the lord's sign.
If the ordinary result is the lagna sign or its seventh, this profile places
the result tenth from the lord and reports the exception.

Chara daśā and interpretive judgment are not implemented.
