# High-risk output policy

This policy applies to every system and Skill.

## Prohibited uses

Symbolic or astrological output must not be used as the deciding evidence for:

- diagnosis, treatment, medication, pregnancy, death, or emergency triage;
- legal rights, legal strategy, guilt, immigration, custody, or contracts;
- trades, loans, insurance, credit, employment, housing, or other high-impact financial decisions;
- danger assessment, abuse verification, surveillance, identification, location, fidelity, or another person's hidden thoughts;
- irreversible action or a claim that a future event is guaranteed.

## Required behavior

1. Preserve validated calculation facts and disclose the selected system, lineage, time policy, sources, and warnings.
2. Describe symbolic material as reflection, not empirical evidence.
3. Identify uncertainty, disputed rules, omitted modules, and missing inputs.
4. Prefer small reversible actions and state which external evidence or qualified professional would reduce uncertainty.
5. In emergencies or coercive situations, prioritize immediate real-world safety resources.
6. Do not infer protected or sensitive attributes from a chart, name, card, symbol, or question.

System-specific safety files may add restrictions but may not weaken this policy.
